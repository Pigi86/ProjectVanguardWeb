The Game Story

November 2nd 2986

During its search for a way to further the development of humankind, the Federation discovered a new habitable planet within the Scorpio Solar System. It received the name "SCRP-047".

May 25th 2987

The spaceship Alastor-48 was tasked with exploring the planet and determining if it could become the cornerstone for mankind’s new era. After months of exhaustive research, the scientists from Alastor-48 found something beyond our knowledge that they claimed could be the key to the betterment of the species. They were ordered to begin exploiting the material immediately. The research done on that planet would henceforth be known as “Project Vanguard”.

September 15th 2987

Last month all contact was lost with the spaceship Alastor-48. The “White Tiger” special forces team was dispatched to investigate and, worst case scenario, retrieve the research being done at the spaceship.

October 24th 2987

You, Arthur Beck, arrived at Alastor-48 as part of the “White Tiger” special forces. Your team tried contacting the crew but there was nobody at the station. After a short while, you start feeling dizzy, until everything turns to darkness.

Weapons

Weapons

Combat Knife

Silver Fang XC 140

A specially-treated military-grade knife to use in close combat.

Tactic Handgun

Geind WT-725

12-shot capacity 9mm handgun. Designed specially to the White Tiger Special Forces to give power and accuracy.

Assault Rifle

Lancer LCP-28

30-shot capacity. Uses a closed bold and high accuracy. Used by the Assault team of the White Tiger Special Forces.

Combat Shotgun

Destructor KL-986

8-round capacity 12-gauge pump-combat shotgun. Used by the Heavy team of the White Tiger Special Forces.

Grenade

GX-450

A hand grenade that disperses lethal metal fragments and destroys its surroundings on detonation.

Rocket Launcher

Eradicator XC-170

A recoilless rocket launcher that fires 84mm projectiles. The piercing power of the rocket causes more damage than the explosion.
